I was a PhD student in the Department of Mathematics at KU Leuven, and I defensed my thesis on March, 2022. My supervisors are Nero Budur and Jean-Baptiste Teyssier.

My thesis is about a conjecture about absolute sets of rigid local system of higher rank. Particularly, I study special subsets of complex local sysetms of higher-ranks and the Riemann-Hilbert morphism between moduli spaces.
